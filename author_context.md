## Repository Purpose & Goals
The primary goal of this repository (`PaddyMathPapers`) is **educational and exploratory**. 
- It serves as a mathematical sandbox to explore ideas the author has had for years, often blending concepts from signal processing, algebra, and differential geometry.
- The intent is to learn new math by formally working through these concepts with AI assistance.
- **Publication Intent:** While the author would be thrilled to eventually publish, it is not the primary driver right now. 
  - **Paper A (Sequence/Entropy):** Primarily exploratory and pedagogical. Any grandiose or overly formal framing was largely an artifact of previous AI-assisted drafting.
  - **Paper B (Spectral Compression):** Shows more practical potential for actual application and possible publication, but the immediate goal remains learning and refining the underlying math (e.g., implementing the Bishop Frame).
